Hard riddles

There are 3 levels of riddles. First there is the easy section. Next there is the medium section. Last but not least there is the hard section.

Answers are at the end of each section.There are 10 questions in the first and second section and in the last one there are 20.

Good luck! Try to answer each question correctly!

Easy Section:-

1. What has to be broken before you can use it?
2. I’m tall when I’m young, and I’m short when I’m old. What am I?
3. What month of the year has 28 days?
4. What is full of holes but still holds water?
5. What question can you never answer yes to?
6. What is always in front of you but can’t be seen?
7. There’s a one-story house in which everything is yellow. Yellow walls, yellow doors, yellow furniture. What colour are the stairs?
8. What can you break, even if you never pick it up or touch it?
9. What goes up but never comes down?
10. A man who was outside in the rain without an umbrella or hat didn’t get a single hair on his head wet. How?

Easy Section (Answers):-

1. An egg

2. A candle
3. All of them
4. A sponge
5. Are you asleep yet?
6. The future
7. There are no stairs, it's a one story house
8. A promise
9. Your age
10. He was bald
